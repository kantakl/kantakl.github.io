Bigfoot Sightings in the United States

Sasquatch, Skunk-Ape, Bigfoot; this elusive creature has become a well-known cryptid in North America over the last 150 years.

"I had two squatches approach me after I had shot a deer in 2013... They were yelling back and forth making sounds I haven't heard in the woods in 35 yrs. They sound like a mix of a bull, a bull elk and a lion. No animal makes sounds like that in Georgia." Jones County, Georgia. 11-05-2013.
